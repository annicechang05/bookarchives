My Top Quotes

These are some of my favorite quotes, pulled from the books in my Top 10. Half of these are Emily Henry because I absolutely adore her and her writing style.

“And that was the moment I realized: when the world felt dark and scary, love could whisk you off to go dancing; laughter could take some of the pain away; beauty could punch holes in your fear. I decided then that my life would be full of all three.”

— Emily Henry, Beach Read

“The best kind of love doesn’t happen on moonlit walks and romantic vacations. It happens in between the folds of everyday life. It’s not grand gestures that show how you feel, it’s all the little secret things you do to make her life better that you never tell her about... Because if it’s this good when life is draining and mundane and hard, think of how wonderful it will be when the love songs are playing and the moon is out.”

— Abby Jimenez, Just For the Summer

“Never trust people who don't have something in their lives that they love beyond all reason.”

— Fredrik Backman, Beartown

“You get a dog and you know two things - you're going to fall in love with it and it's going to die one day. You knowingly walk headfirst into a heartbreak. That's the basic madness of dog ownership.”

— Annabel Monaghan, Summer Romance

"In every universe, it's you for me. Even if it's not me for you.”

— Emily Henry, Happy Place

“What an uncomfortable, terrible source of shame it is for the world that the victim is so often the one left with the most empathy for others.”

— Fredrik Backman, Beartown

“I’ve never met someone who is so perfectly my favorite person.”

— Emily Henry, Beach Read

“You're like gravity. Everything keeps spinning, but my mind's always got one hand on you.”

— Emily Henry, Happy Place
